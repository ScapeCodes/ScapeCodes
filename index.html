F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can i download your plugins?

All my plugins can be easily downloaded or purchased on my spigot page. https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/authors/devscape.1570420/

What versions do your plugins support?

All my plugins are designed to support versions from 1.8 to the latest. I'll do my best to keep them consistently up to date.

Are your plugins open sourced?

Only my free plugins are open source, you can find them on my github. https://github.com/ScapeCodes/

I have an error or found a bug, where can i post it?

The best place to report issues and bugs is through my Discord. It's well-organized for each plugin, making it easy to get support. However, please be patient, as I'm not coding every day.

Will you release new plugins?

Absolutely, I'm always eager to take on new projects.

Does your plugins have a wiki?

Not at the moment, but it's something I plan to release in the future. Unfortunately, I’m short on time right now
